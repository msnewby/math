How to split shapes into equal parts!

🍕 You and your friends want to share a pizza FAIRLY.
How do you cut it so everyone gets the SAME amount?
2 Equal Shares

💡 When we cut a shape into pieces that are ALL THE SAME SIZE — those are called EQUAL SHARES!
📖 Words We Need to Know
Whole
The entire shape — before you divide it
Share / Part
One equal piece of the shape
Equal
All pieces are the SAME size
Halves
2 equal shares
"Half and half!"
Thirds
3 equal shares
Fourths
4 equal shares
"Also called quarters!"
🧠 More shares = SMALLER pieces | Fewer shares = BIGGER pieces
