📖 Words We Need to Know
More Than
Signal phrase = ADD
"16 more than 188" → 188 + 16
Less Than
Signal phrase = SUBTRACT
"16 less than 188" → 188 − 16
Regroup
When a column adds up to 10+, carry the 1 to the next column
Place Value
Hundreds, Tens, Ones — line up digits before adding!
Sum
The answer when you add — 188 + 16 = 204
🚦 "MORE THAN" → ADD | "LESS THAN" → SUBTRACT | Always translate the words first!
