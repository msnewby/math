📖 Words We Need to Know
Hour Hand
The SHORT hand 🔴 — it tells the HOUR
Look which number it just passed!
Minute Hand
The LONG hand 🔵 — count by 5s around the clock to find the minutes
Skip Count by 5
5, 10, 15, 20, 25... each clock number = 5 more minutes
Digital Time
Hours : Minutes written with numbers — always 2 digits for minutes (2:05, not 2:5)
A.M. / P.M.
A.M. = morning. P.M. = afternoon/evening. Clocks repeat twice a day!
🔴 Short = Hour (past which number?)  |  🔵 Long = Minutes (count by 5s)
