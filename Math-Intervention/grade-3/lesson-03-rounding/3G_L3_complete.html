What is Rounding?

Rounding means finding the closest round number to our number.

💡 Think of it like this…

Is 559 closer to 550 or 560?
Rounding helps us find out!

Round numbers end in 0 (like 10, 20, 100, 200…)

Rounding to the Nearest 10

🔎 Look at the ONES digit

🔴 0, 1, 2, 3, 4 → Round DOWN

🟩 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 → Round UP

559

Ones = 9 → round UP
✅ 560

243

Ones = 3 → round DOWN
✅ 240

Rounding to the Nearest 100

🔎 Look at the TENS digit

🔴 0, 1, 2, 3, 4 → Round DOWN

🟩 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 → Round UP

559

Tens = 5 → round UP
✅ 600

243

Tens = 4 → round DOWN
✅ 200

🗝 Vocabulary Key — Rounding to the Nearest 10 and 100

3G · Lesson 3 · Acadiance Applied Math · 3rd Grade
TEACHER COPY
Round / Rounding
Definition

Finding the closest multiple of 10 or 100 to a number.

Example

559 rounded to nearest 10 = 560

Nearest 10
Definition

The multiple of 10 closest to a number. Look at the ONES digit.

Example

673 → ones is 3 → round down → 670

Nearest 100
Definition

The multiple of 100 closest to a number. Look at the TENS digit.

Example

771 → tens is 7 → round up → 800

Ones Place
Definition

The digit in the far right of a number. Used when rounding to nearest 10.

Example

In 243, the ones digit is 3.

Tens Place
Definition

The second digit from the right. Used when rounding to nearest 100.

Example

In 243, the tens digit is 4.
