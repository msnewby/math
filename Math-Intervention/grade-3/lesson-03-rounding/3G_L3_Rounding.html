What Is Rounding?

💡 Rounding = Finding the CLOSEST round number

Round numbers end in zero — like 10, 20, 50, 100, 200, 500…

We round when we want an estimate that is easy to work with.

◎
Round numbers:
10, 20, 30, 40, 50…
100, 200, 300…
⏳
NOT round numbers:
13, 27, 45, 98…
112, 347, 559…
The Golden Rule 🎉
5+
Round UP
🟩 add 1
OR
0–4
Round DOWN
🔴 keep it

🔎 Which digit do you look at?

Rounding to nearest 10 → look at the ONES digit
Rounding to nearest 100 → look at the TENS digit
