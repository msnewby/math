🖐️ Practice Strategies!
📄
Fold Paper
Fold a square into 4 equal parts — shade 1 and write the fraction!
🖍️
Color & Write
Teacher draws a shape — student colors some parts and writes the fraction!
🍕
Real Life
Pizza has 4 slices — you eat 1. What fraction did you eat? 1/4!
✌️
Two Fingers
Point to each part and count. Then hold up fingers for shaded. Write it!
✅ Always check: are ALL the parts the SAME size? If not, it's not a fair fraction!
