📖 The 4 Rules for Reading a Clock
🔴 Short Hand = HOUR
Find it first. Which number has it just PASSED? That's your hour.
🔵 Long Hand = MINUTES
Count by 5s around the clock to where the long hand points.
🔢 Write Hour First
Hour THEN colon THEN minutes: 9:15 — never swap them!
✍️ Always 2 Digits for Minutes
Write 9:05 — NOT 9:5. Minutes always get 2 digits!
🧠 Skip count by 5s around the clock: 5·10·15·20·25·30·35·40·45·50·55·00
