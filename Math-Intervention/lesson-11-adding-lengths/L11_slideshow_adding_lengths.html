🪢 There are 2 ropes. One rope is 14 inches and the other rope is 7 inches. How many inches are both ropes together?
Rope 1
14 inches
14 in.
Rope 2
7 inches
7 in.
Total
21 inches total!
21 in.
💡 "Together" is the signal word — it means ADD the lengths! 14 + 7 = 21 inches
📖 Words We Need to Know
Length
How long an object is — measured with a ruler in inches
Together
Signal word = ADD the lengths!
"both together" → add
Total Length
The sum of two or more lengths added together
Units
Always say INCHES in your answer! The unit tells what we measured in.
Bar Model
A picture that shows lengths as bars — helps you see the addition!
🚦 Signal words for adding lengths: "together" · "combined" · "in all" · "total length" → ADD!

🖐️ Let's Use Our Hands!
📏

Ruler + String

Cut two pieces of string (14 in. + 7 in.), tape them end to end, measure the total!

🧱

Linking Cubes

Build a train of 14 cubes and one of 7 cubes. Connect them — count how many total!

📏

Number Line Jump

Start at 14 on the number line. Jump 7 more spaces. Where do you land?

📊

Draw Bar Models

Draw two bars of different lengths. Label each. Draw a total bar and label it!

📐 Always write INCHES in your answer! "21" alone isn't complete — it's "21 INCHES"!
