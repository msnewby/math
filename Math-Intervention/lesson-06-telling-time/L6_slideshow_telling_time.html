💡 Every clock has TWO hands — a SHORT one for hours and a LONG one for minutes!
📖 Words We Need to Know
Hour Hand
The SHORT hand — points to the HOUR
🔴 Think: short = hour
Minute Hand
The LONG hand — points to the MINUTES
🔵 Think: long = minute (longer word!)
Analog Clock
A clock with two hands that move around a circle face
Digital Clock
A clock that shows time with numbers only — like 2:45
Minute
Each number on the clock = 5 minutes
Skip count by 5s around the clock!
🧠 Memory trick: SHORT hand = HOUR (both short words!) LONG hand = MINUTE (both longer words!)
👀 Watch Me — Draw 2:45
2:45
Hour hand (short)
Minute hand (long)
1

Read the digital time

It says 2:45 → hour is 2, minutes are 45

2

Draw the MINUTE hand first (long 🔵)

45 minutes → count by 5s: 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, 45 = the 9! Point long hand at 9.

3

Draw the HOUR hand (short 🔴)

It's 2:45 — almost 3:00, so hour hand is between 2 and 3, closer to 3.

✅

Check your work!

Long blue hand → 9. Short red hand → between 2 and 3. Done!

🔢 The Skip Counting Trick!
Each number on the clock = 5 minutes!
🎯 For 2:45 → the 9 on the clock = 45 minutes! LONG hand points to 9!

Color Coding
