📖 Words We Need to Know
Addend
The numbers being ADDED together
In 5+5+6, the addends are 5, 5, and 6
Sum
The ANSWER to an addition problem
5 + 5 + 6 = 16 ← the sum
Doubles
Adding a number to ITSELF
5 + 5 = 10 is a doubles fact!
Make a Ten
Find two addends that add to 10 FIRST, then add the third

🧠 Two Smart Strategies!

🎯 Strategy 1: DOUBLES

5 + 5 + 6

Spot two numbers that are the SAME. Add them first → they make 10! Then add the third.

5+5=10 → 10+6=16 ✅

🔟 Strategy 2: MAKE A TEN

8 + 2 + 5

Find two numbers that ADD UP TO 10. Add those first, then add the third number.

8+2=10 → 10+5=15 ✅

➡️ Strategy 3: LEFT TO RIGHT

3 + 4 + 7

When no doubles or tens, just add left to right. First two numbers, then add the third.

3+4=7 → 7+7=14 ✅
🙌 Always check for DOUBLES or MAKE A TEN first — they make the math SO much easier!
