📖 Words We Need to Know
Word Problem
A math STORY that asks you to solve something with numbers
Signal Word
A word that tells you WHICH operation to use — "in all" means ADD!
Addend
The numbers being added together
3 + 4 → 3 and 4 are addends
Sum / Total
The ANSWER — the whole amount
3 + 4 = 7 ← the sum
In All
"How many IN ALL?" means add everything together!
🚦 SIGNAL WORDS for addition: "in all" · "in total" · "altogether" · "how many" · "combined"

🖐️ Let's Use Our Hands!
🔵

Counters

Use two colors of counters — one color per group. Count them all together!

✏️

Draw a Picture

Draw circles or tallies for each group, then count the total!

👆

Count On

Start at the bigger number, count on your fingers for the smaller number!

📏

Number Line

Start at 3, jump 4 more spaces. Where do you land?
