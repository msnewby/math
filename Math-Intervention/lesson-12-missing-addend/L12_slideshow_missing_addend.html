📖 Words We Need to Know
Missing Addend
The UNKNOWN number in the equation — shown as ___ or ?
Unknown
The number we don't know yet — the one we're solving for
Fact Family
Related facts: 9+5=14, 5+9=14, 14-5=9, 14-9=5
Total
The sum — the biggest number in the equation
Subtract to Find
Total − Known addend = Missing addend
🔑 KEY FORMULA: Total − Known = Missing | Then CHECK: Missing + Known = Total ✅
