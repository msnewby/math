📏 How long is your desk?
How long is your pencil?
How do we KNOW for sure?
This is a RULER — our measuring tool today!
💡 A ruler helps us measure exactly how long something is — in INCHES!
📖 Words We Need to Know
Ruler
A tool we use to measure how long something is
Inch
A unit of length — rulers show inches with numbers 1–12
Length
How LONG something is from one end to the other
Zero End
Always start measuring from the 0 end of the ruler!
Measure
To find out exactly how long, tall, or wide something is
Estimate
A smart GUESS about how long something is before you measure
📌 BIG RULE: Always line up ONE END of the object at ZERO on the ruler!
👀 Watch Me — Just Like the Test!
The line from the test — where does it end?
The line is 11 inches long! ✅
1

Find the ZERO end

Line up the LEFT end of the line at 0 on the ruler.

2

Find where it ENDS

Look at the right end of the line. What number is it pointing to?

3

Read the number

The line ends at 11 — so it is 11 inches long!

✏️

Write your answer

Always write the number AND the unit: 11 inches

🖐️ Let's Use Our Hands!

Real Rulers

🧱

Linking Cubes

Each cube ≈ 1 inch! Build a "ruler" of 5 cubes, then measure objects.

🧵

String

Cut a piece of string. Estimate how long it is, THEN measure with ruler.

✏️

Classroom Hunt

Find something 3 inches, 6 inches, and 12 inches long in the room!

⚠️ COMMON MISTAKE: Don't start at the 1! Always start at ZERO!
